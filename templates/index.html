Methodology
1
Analog Day Selection
Historical days scored by peak shape, energy, ramp profile, downstate load, weather (HDH), and recency. Inverse-distance weighted sampling probabilities.
2
Gaussian Copula Fitting
264-dimensional (11 zones × 24 hours) Gaussian copula fitted to rank-transformed forecast errors using Ledoit-Wolf shrinkage estimator for the correlation matrix.
3
Scenario Generation
Cholesky-based correlated Normal draws mapped back to load via probability-weighted empirical quantile: L = Forecast / (1 + ε).
